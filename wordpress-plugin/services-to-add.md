## NOTES

- **Price** — fill in the actual prices from your current WooCommerce store
- **Sale Price** — only fill in if the service is currently discounted
- **Stripe Price ID** — leave blank for now, we'll fill these in when we connect Stripe
- **Featured Image** — use the same images from your current WooCommerce products
- **Description** — copy from your existing WooCommerce product descriptions to save time
- You don't have to add everything at once — start with your top 20 most popular services
